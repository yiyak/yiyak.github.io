《蝶恋花*中国》

(Butterfly loves flower · China)


寒意勒花风卷絮，一曲情怀，已断春归去。不觉山中难醉里，多少林同了处起。

帘卷波回千百转，雁字眉峰，旧日催人度。何似当年后心事，万岁风吹破月见。


This Song Iambics is generated based on its “China” cue phrase.






新春快乐

(Happy New Year)


新为清风老，春来是小朝。快出诗语尽，乐却要如高。


Putting the first word of every sentence above together is "新春快乐" , which means "happy new year". The Acrostic Poems is generated based on this “happy new year” cue phrase.
